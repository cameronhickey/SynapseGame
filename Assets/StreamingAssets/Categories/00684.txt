THE 7 ANCIENT WONDERS
200|c. 650 A.D. Arabs invading Egypt stole the upper blocks from these monuments to construct new buildings|the pyramids
400|This wonder lives on in our vocabulary as the term for a large, stately tomb|the mausoleum
600|One legend says Nebuchadnezzar II built this wonder to cheer up his homesick wife|the Hanging Gardens of Babylon
800|The shortest-lived of the Wonders was this enormous statue of Helios, which stood for only 56 years|the Colossus of Rhodes
1000|After Phidias, the original sculptor, died, his descendants maintained this wonder at Olympia|the statue of Zeus at Olympia
