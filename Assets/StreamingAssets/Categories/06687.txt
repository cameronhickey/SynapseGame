400|This word for the building where legislatures meet comes from the hill in Rome where the Temple of Jupiter stood|capitol
600|A eulogy of Dolley Madison by President Taylor in 1849 gave us this 2-word term|First Lady
800|This word comes from the name of a person & the voting district he created that looked like a salamander|gerrymander
1000|This term for an election item comes from the small balls Italian citizens used as a method of voting|ballots
