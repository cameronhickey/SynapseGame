BIBLICAL FASHION
200|After realizing they were naked, Adam & Eve sewed these leaves together & made aprons|Fig Leaves
400|Envious of Joseph, his brothers stripped him of this garment & cast him into a pit|Coat (of many colors)
600|Achan admitted to Joshua that from the spoils of this city he took a "Goodly Babylon Garment\"|Jericho
800|It was the garb of the penitent & those in mourning & was often worn with ashes|Sackcloth
1000|Golden bells & pomegranates were to adorn the hem of the robe of this brother of Moses|Aaron
