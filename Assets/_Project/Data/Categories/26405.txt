WINEMAKER'S GLOSSARY
200|Retsina is a Greek white or rosé wine flavored with the resin from this cone-producing evergreen (sounds delish!)|pine
400|From Latin for "heavy", this 4-letter word refers to a style of dry champagne|brut
600|Think it over—Glühwein is a German wine that has gone through this heating & spicing process|mulling (mulled)
800|Red wine, fruit juice, spices & sugar go into this Spanish offering sometimes served as an iced punch|sangria
1000|This verb refers to adding extra alcohol to wine to produce liquors like port or sherry|fortify
