RELIGION BY THE NUMBERS
200|This legendary dozen included 2 Jameses, 2 Judases & an eventual replacement named Matthias|Twelve Apostles/disciples
400|The sixth of these was an outbreak of boils & sores|Ten Plagues of Egypt
600|It's the Greek term meaning "5 tools" that represents the 1st 5 books of the Bible|Pentateuch
800|For Muslims: witnessing, prayer, alms giving, fasting & pilgrimage|Five Pillars of Faith
1000|Right effort, right speech & right action are 3 parts of this noble Buddhist way|Eightfold Path
