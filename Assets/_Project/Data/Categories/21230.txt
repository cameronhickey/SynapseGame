WHAT DO YOUR SHOES MEAN?
200|A diminutive of the Italian word for "dagger" gives us the name of these heels|a stiletto
400|A laced shoe as well as a shirting fabric is named for this British university town|Oxford
600|A girl in the Buster Brown comic strip gave her name to these girls' shoes that have a strap over the instep|Mary Janes
800|A word for a type of rope gives us the name of these rope-soled sandals|espadrille
1000|Queen Victoria's Scottish castle is the source of the name of this laced shoe|Balmoral
