WORLD OF EMPIRES
200|Sacrum Romanum Imperium in Latin, it was ruled mainly by Frankish & German kings for roughly 10 centuries|the Holy Roman Empire

800|Around 27 B.C., the Pax Romana began during the reign of this emperor & would last for 200 years|Caesar Augustus
1000|In 1747 the Durrani Empire's capital was in this Afghan city once taken by Alexander the Great, who was also called Iskander|Kandahar
