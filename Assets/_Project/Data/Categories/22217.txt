GLOSSARY OF HISTORY
200|Once used to authenticate documents, it's a device to leave an impression, usually in wax, or the impression itself|a seal
400|A physical repository of historic documents; there's a famous one at 700 Pennsylvania Avenue|an archive
600|In this type of manuscript, the handwritten text is supplemented by the addition of elaborate illustration|an illuminated manuscript
800|Term for the cultural & artistic movement of the late 18th & early 19th C. emphasizing emotion & sentiment rather than reason|Romantic
1000|It's the system by which coats of arms & other family or national emblems are devised & regulated|heraldry
