DESCRIBING THE OLYMPIC EVENT
200|Perform for 9.58 seconds; tie world record set in 2009; point to sky|the 100-meters
400|Lie on your back on the 50-pound namesake item; slide into history at about 90 mph|a luge
600|Be unbelievably proficient with a rope, hoop, ball, clubs & ribbon|rhythmic gymnastics
800|Easily confused with a body part; row, row, row your boat with an oar in each hand|sculling
1000|Your fencing foe must get the point (not the edge), mask to feet; no translation needed at the Paris Games|épée
