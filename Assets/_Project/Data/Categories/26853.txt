SEVEN WONDERS
200|The first of the 7 wonders to be built|the Great Pyramid (the Pyramids of Egypt)
400|In the Middle Ages the Arabs replaced its beacon with a small mosque|the lighthouse at Alexandria
600|Ancient writers described them as a series of terraces rising along the Euphrates River|the Hanging Gardens of Babylon
800|Said to be 105 feet tall, this bronze statue of Helios overlooked an island harbor|the Colossus of Rhodes
1000|The temple at Ephesus was dedicated to this goddess of the hunt|Diana (or Artemis)
