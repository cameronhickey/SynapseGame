A PREFIX MENU
200|From Greek for "on both sides", it precedes -bious & -theater|amphi
400|Meaning "against", it goes before -band to describe something against the law|contra
600|Arabic for "the", it's been incorporated into many math & science words|al
800|One of the few prefixes from German, it means "super" & now stands on its own as a proper noun|uber
1000|All meaning "half", these 3 rhyming prefixes combine before -quaver to describe a very, very short musical note|demi, semi & hemi
