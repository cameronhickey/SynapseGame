HOW DO YOU WORK THIS THING?
200|Wind 12-18" of it around your index fingers, slip it between 2 teeth to the gum & scrape both sides|dental floss
400|Put the point at the center of the desired circle & swing the pencil around it|a compass
600|To add 14 to another number, shift 4 beads on the 1s wire & 1 bead on the 10s wire|an abacus
800|Hammer it into a crack in the rock & clip a carabiner to its eye through which to run rope|a piton
1000|Place the electric pads to the chest, press down, yell "Clear!" & hit the button|defibrillator
