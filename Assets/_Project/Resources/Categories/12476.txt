WORLD CAPITAL HISTORY
200|This city was just a regional capital before Kublai Khan made it, for the first time, the political center of the nation|Beijing
400|The military & administrative center of the Aztecs became this capital of the colony of New Spain|Mexico City
600|An oasis that was the residence of the Ibn Saud family, it became the capital when the country was formed in 1932|Riyadh
800|This North African city was once a Roman fortress named Babylon; the Mamelukes made it an African capital|Cairo
1000|Prospering under Tatar rule & expanding as it intersected trade routes, this city became a capital in the 15th century|Moscow
