200|Because Bernardo Strozzi had been a Capuchin friar, he was nicknamed this, which sounds like an Italian coffee drink|Cappuccino
400|Luca Giordano was nicknamed "Luca Fa Presto" because he worked this way|fast
600|Donato di Niccolo found fame as a Renaissance sculptor using this name (later taken by a Ninja Turtle)|Donatello
800|First & last name of the "Garden of Earthly Delights" painter; he took the name from the town of 'S Hertogenbosch|Hieronymus Bosch
1000|The surname of this "Primavera" painter came from his brother's nickname, which meant "little barrel\"|Sandro Botticelli
