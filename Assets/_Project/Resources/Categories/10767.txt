THE HISTORY OF MEDICINE
200|400s B.C.: This Greek "father of medicine" bases medicine on observation & reasoning|Hippocrates
400|1590: Dutch lens grinder Zacharias Janssen invents a type of this optical research instrument|a microscope
600|1948: Johns Hopkins scientists find that this antihistamine alleviates motion sickness|dramamine
800|1952: Paul Zoll develops the first of these cardiac aids that help control an irregular heartbeat|a pacemaker
1000|1844: During a tooth extraction, Dr. Horace Wells makes the first use of this gas as an anesthetic|nitrous oxide (laughing gas)
