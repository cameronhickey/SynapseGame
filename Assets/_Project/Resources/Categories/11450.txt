DIRE STRAITS
200|The name of this famed rock & the strait beneath it come from the name of a Berber slave|Gibraltar
400|The Bab el Mandeb Strait connects the Gulf of Aden to this "colorful" sea|the Red Sea
600|A busy shipping lane, the Strait of Malacca between Sumatra & the Malay Peninsula, links these 2 oceans|the Indian & the Pacific
800|Named after navigator John & not his son Sebastian, this strait joins the Atlantic to the St. Lawrence Gulf|Cabot
1000|Byzantium levied a toll on ships using this strait that opens into the Black Sea|the Bosporus
